SavannaClick has been developed by the Environmental Change Biology lab in the School of Biological Sciences at the University of Tasmania. Log in using the Facebook login button at the top to begin helping us classify the savanna ecosystems of the world, so better understand their association with raiinforest systems, and to help tune global vegetation models.
